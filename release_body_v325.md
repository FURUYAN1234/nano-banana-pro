# Nano Banana Pro v3.25-alpha: High-Quality Standing Poses Integration

## 🚀 English

- **[Prompt/Visuals] Integrated Advanced Standing Pose Techniques**
  Based on industry best-practice spatial representation techniques, we have integrated advanced posture instructions into both the system's internal image generation prompt and the Single-Illustration Copy Button. By adding highly compressed instructions for "dynamic contrapposto weight-shift" and "keeping toes aligned together," the system now actively prevents messy foot generation and creates much more natural, attractive, and anatomically stable standing postures.
